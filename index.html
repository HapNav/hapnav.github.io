What is HapNav?

HapNav is the solution to safe bike riding in a busy city. The small navigation device pairs with your bluetooth-enabled smartphone to give you GPS turning and location information by vibrating your bike's handlebars.

HapNav is being developed by a team of students for their Computer Engineering Capstone project at the University of Ottawa.

The Developers
